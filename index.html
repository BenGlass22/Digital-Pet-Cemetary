Digital Pet Cemetary

Welcome to the Digital Pet Cemetary, where we lay our beloved digital pets, avatars and personas to rest. Finally be at peace with those you have lost, being able to lay them to rest is cyber space. No more need to feel guilt over forgotten passwords, accounts and data long deleted. At the Digital Pet Cemetary we provide a place for people to not only mourn the dead, but allow a part of them to exist long into the future. We accepts all types of digital entities and provide users with a unique web address to which they can visit and honor their late digital companions. Visitors are welcome to visit individual grave-sites to pay respect as well as browse through these sites at random.
